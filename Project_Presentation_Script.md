# 🎬 SkillSync AI: The "Claims vs. Reality" Presentation Script

Use this version to align perfectly with the Catalyst Problem Statement.

---

## 1. The Hook & Problem (0:00 - 0:45)
"Hi everyone, I’m Mokshagna. We’ve all seen it: A resume tells you what someone **claims** to know—but it never tells you how well they **actually** know it. 

For companies, this leads to expensive hiring mistakes. For students and job-seekers, it leads to a 'black hole' of rejection with zero feedback. We built **SkillSync AI** to bridge this gap between claims and reality."

## 2. The Solution: Two Sides of the Coin (0:45 - 1:15)
"SkillSync is a dual-purpose AI Agent. 
- **For Companies**: It’s an elite technical screener that uses Jarvis to verify proficiency through conversation.
- **For Candidates**: It’s a personalized mentor. If Jarvis finds a gap, he doesn't just reject you—he builds you a curated upskilling roadmap with real resources."

## 3. The Deep-Dive Assessment (1:15 - 2:15)
"Let’s look at the workflow. We start by cross-referencing the Resume with the Job Description. But we don't stop at a 'Match Score.' 

We trigger **Jarvis**. Jarvis uses a **Deterministic Agentic Loop**. He takes the skill gaps identified in the ATS phase and turns them into a 10-question technical interrogation. 

Notice the **Live Pulse Meter**. This is the 'Real-time Proficiency' indicator. It fluctuates based on technical depth, not just keywords. This is how we overcome the 'claims' on a resume—by forcing a real-world technical conversation."

## 4. The Intelligence & Architecture (2:15 - 2:45)
"Under the hood, we use a **90/10 Weighted Scoring Logic**. 
- 10% comes from the initial ATS alignment.
- 90% comes from the actual interview performance.

This ensures that a 'perfect resume' can't beat a 'perfect technician.' We built this using **Next.js 15** and **Gemini 1.5 Flash**, utilizing a strict system prompt architecture to ensure zero hallucinations and 100% fair evaluation."

## 5. The Final Roadmap (2:45 - 3:15)
"Finally, the **Talent Intelligence Dashboard**. Whether you are a recruiter looking at a candidate's Radar Chart or a student looking at your gap analysis, you get a clear, data-driven picture of where you stand and exactly how to reach the next level. 

SkillSync AI: Making technical proficiency transparent for everyone. Thank you!"
